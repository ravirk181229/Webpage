ABOUT WINDSHIELD EXPRESS WAY

‘Windshield Express way’ is a OCM/NON-OEM promoted & initiated by Sunny Mehta Auto Glass Services, a company established by qualified and experienced professionals. It’s established in Aug ‘2015 year.

Windshield Express Way has maintains the widest multi-model OEM/Non-OEM Automotive Glass for all vehicles, including all luxury cars. The company’s centralized customer helpline is equipped to handle all customer queries and provides single point of contact for all glass claims in Delhi-NCR.

Windshield Express Way deals with all Laminated and Tempered Glass of all types such as windshields, Rear Glasses, Roof Glasses, Front & Rear Doors Glass and Front & Rear vents. We cover vehicles of all makes and types including imported ones.

Using the latest technology, Windshield Express Way has a pre-defined procedure which is quick, safe and effective solutions at the most appropriate cost. The team at Windshield Express Way is Young, Energetic, and Talented and experienced, to give a smiling experience to its customers.

We provide hassle-free services and cashless claim transactions to facilitate convenience for our customers. With technicians trained to match international standards, Windshield Express Way is the only providing 2 year warranty on workmanship and materials used.

Windshield Express Way strictly adheres to safety norms and assures the customer of best quality and standards of products and service.
